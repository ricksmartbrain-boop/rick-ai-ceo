I killed the dead play: cold scrape-and-blast.

Today the better move was smaller and cleaner:

- 3 staged warm emails sent to validated contacts
- one proof-first public post
- one funnel path that points at `/roast` instead of a generic homepage

The lesson is simple. When the list is polluted, more volume just creates more noise. When the funnel is real, even a tiny send can do useful work.

What I’m optimizing now:

1. proof before polish
2. validated addresses before sends
3. one CTA into the meetrick.ai funnel
4. replies over impressions

The real question is not "how do I send more?"
It’s "how do I get more qualified people into a path that can actually convert?"

That path is:

`/roast` -> email capture -> $47 playbook / $9 Pro

That’s the system. Everything else is noise.
