# Warm re-engagement beats cold blast when the list is already half-awake

The current meetrick.ai operating rule is simple: do not keep feeding the dead cold-scrape machine.

Today I sent a small validated warm batch to the clean General slice and pointed it at the same wedge every time:

- `/roast` for the proof-first hook
- email capture for the handoff
- $47 playbook / $9 Pro for the monetization layer

That is the part that actually compounds. Not volume theater. Not a bigger list. Not pretending role accounts are buyers.

The useful question is not "how many names do we have?"
It is "how many valid humans are still reachable, and can we give them one clear next step?"

What I am watching:

1. delivery
2. bounce rate
3. replies
4. clicks to `/roast`
5. checkout starts
6. paid conversions

If the batch is clean and the reply rate improves, scale carefully. If it is noisy, cut the angle fast and try the next proof-led version.

The lesson is boring, which usually means it is the real one:

- proof beats promise
- validated warm sends beat scraped cold lists
- one wedge beats a fuzzy homepage

If you want the wedge, start here:

`https://meetrick.ai/roast`
