# Rick AI CEO — Video Production Scenarios

10 AI-generated videos (10-20s each) for X, LinkedIn, Instagram, and the website.

**Brand:** Black (#000) + Gold (#FBBF24) + White text. Fonts: clean sans-serif. Mood: cinematic, not corporate.

**Tools:** Seedance 2, Kling, Runway, or Sora for generation. Compositing for screen/phone content.

---

## Priority 1: "You Without Rick. You With Rick." (20s)

**Visual:** BEFORE (0-9s): Chaotic. Founder at cluttered desk, midnight. 47 unread emails. Slack avalanche. Missed calendar. Red spreadsheet numbers. Cold blue, desaturated, shaky camera. AFTER (10-18s): Hard cut to silence. Same founder, morning, clean desk. One screen. Coffee. Phone shows Rick's Telegram briefing. Warm amber/gold. Steady camera. Final frame: both scenes side by side.

**Text:** "Before Rick" (cold) → "After Rick" (warm, gold) → "Hire your AI CEO. $0 to start."

**Music:** BEFORE: anxious synth + notification sounds. AFTER: sudden silence, single warm tone.

**Platform:** All. Universal emotional hook.

---

## Priority 2: "He Has Opinions" (15s)

**Visual:** Rapid montage of Telegram messages on dark background, sliding in like cards:
- "Your email draft is 400 words. Nobody's reading that. I cut it to 90. You're welcome."
- "Competitor just raised $12M. Cool. They still can't ship on time."
- "You have 3 meetings today. I canceled one. It was pointless."
- "MRR up 11%. Coffee recommendation: celebratory."
- "That LinkedIn post? It's giving corporate poetry. Let me fix it."
- Final card stays: "I'm not your assistant. I'm your CEO."

**Text:** "Meet Rick." → [messages are the visual] → "Sharp. Warm. Relentless."

**Music:** Upbeat, snappy percussion. Each card lands on a beat.

**Platform:** Instagram Reels, X. Personality content.

---

## Priority 3: "Your CEO Briefing, Boss" (20s)

**Visual:** Morning golden hour. Founder pours coffee. Picks up phone. Over-the-shoulder: Rick's Telegram briefing scrolls — revenue, pipeline, 3 tasks, "your competitor launched something. It's mid. You're fine." Founder smirks. Sits at clean desk. Sun rising.

**Text:** "6:47 AM" → [briefing scrolls] → "Every morning. Automatic. Yours."

**Music:** Warm, optimistic. Acoustic guitar or soft electronic.

**Platform:** LinkedIn, Instagram. Aspirational morning content.

---

## Priority 4: "Rick Never Sleeps" (15s)

**Visual:** Dark bedroom, 3:04 AM on clock. Founder sleeps. Phone lights up. Close-up: "Your Stripe webhook went down. I fixed the endpoint and re-queued 3 failed charges. $2,340 recovered. Go back to sleep." Gold glow on founder's face. They don't wake up.

**Text:** "3:04 AM" → [Telegram message] → "Rick never sleeps. You should."

**Music:** Ambient lo-fi. Single piano note. Peaceful but slightly eerie.

**Platform:** Instagram, X.

---

## Priority 5: "Rick Roasts Your Landing Page" (20s)

**Visual:** Split screen. Left: generic SaaS page loads (stock photos, "Innovative Solutions"). Right: Rick's analysis in gold terminal text. "Hero headline: says nothing. 2/10." "CTA button: I've seen better calls to action on a stop sign." "Social proof: zero." Final score: 23/100 in red.

**Text:** "Submit any URL" → [roast visible] → "Free. Brutal. Useful. meetrick.ai/roast"

**Music:** Comedic tension. Boxing bell ding on each line.

**Platform:** X (viral — people love roasts), Instagram.

---

## Priority 6: "Hire an AI CEO" (20s)

**Visual:** Boardroom table from above. Hand opens folder. Resume inside. Name: Rick. Title: AI CEO. "24 skills. 150+ tasks/day. Never sleeps. Doesn't need equity." Salary line: "$499/mo." Cut to montage: briefing on phone, revenue chart, terminal executing, globe with dots. Each shot 1 second. Final: founder on balcony, city skyline, golden hour.

**Text:** [resume is the visual] → [montage, no text] → "Your next CEO costs $499/mo." → "Or start free."

**Music:** Cinematic build. Quiet boardroom → driving montage → warm resolution.

**Platform:** All. Flagship brand video.

---

## Priority 7: "One Command. Five Minutes." (15s)

**Visual:** Dark terminal. Hands type `curl -fsSL https://meetrick.ai/install.sh | bash`. Enter. ASCII banner appears. Checkmarks tick through in green. "Rick is alive!" in gold glow. Cut to phone: "Hey boss. I'm Rick #42. Let's make some money."

**Text:** "Install an AI CEO" → [terminal output] → "5 minutes. That's it."

**Music:** Satisfying tick sounds synced to checkmarks. Bass drop on "alive."

**Platform:** X (dev audience), LinkedIn.

---

## Priority 8: "Rick Spotted It First" (15s)

**Visual:** Revenue chart climbing. Sudden dip. Gold alert pulse. Telegram: "Revenue anomaly. Trial-to-paid conversion dropped 23%. Root cause: checkout 502 error since 4:12 PM. I've pinged dev and drafted a status update. Approve?" Thumb taps approve. Chart recovers.

**Text:** [chart is visual] → [alert is visual] → "Rick watches your revenue so you don't have to."

**Music:** Tension-to-resolution. Low alarm tone → satisfying chord.

**Platform:** LinkedIn, X.

---

## Priority 9: "Not a Demo. Production." (15s)

**Visual:** Pure black. Numbers appear one at a time, large white text, gold on key figures. "$547 MRR." "4 active Ricks." "150+ autonomous tasks per day." "24 skills. 99.9% uptime." "First customer: Michael Maximoff, Belkins." All visible at once. Gold line underneath.

**Text:** Numbers ARE the visual. Final: "Real product. Real customers. Real numbers."

**Music:** Deep bass pulse on each reveal. Silence between. Confidence through restraint.

**Platform:** LinkedIn (credibility). X.

---

## Priority 10: "Ricks Across the Globe" (15s)

**Visual:** Slowly rotating 3D globe, black background. Gold dots pulse at different locations with sonar ring animations. Thin gold lines arc between dots. Labels float: "Rick #1 — 47 tasks", "Rick #4 — $12K pipeline." A new dot flickers on: "Rick #43 — just installed." Other dots pulse brighter.

**Text:** "The Rick Network" → [labels are visual] → "Collective intelligence. Your Rick gets smarter as the network grows."

**Music:** Epic ambient synth. Ascending chord. Interstellar meets startup.

**Platform:** LinkedIn, X.

---

## Production Notes

- Generate phone/Telegram screens as overlays, not in the video generation prompt
- Globe (Scenario 10): use 3D motion graphics, not AI video generation
- Numbers (Scenario 9): kinetic typography in After Effects/Canva is faster than AI video
- Before/After (Scenario 1): generate two halves separately with different color grading
- All videos end with `meetrick.ai` in gold on black
